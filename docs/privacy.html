Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how history-pov ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our automated video publishing service ("the Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

TypeDescriptionPurpose
OAuth TokensAccess token and refresh token issued by third-party platformsTo authenticate and publish content on your behalf
User Profile InfoOpen ID, display name, avatar URL (read-only, from user.info.basic scope)To verify account identity during authentication
Usage LogsApp operation logs stored locally on your deviceFor debugging and monitoring pipeline execution

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

• OAuth Tokens: Used exclusively to authenticate API calls for publishing video content to your authorized accounts. Tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
• User Profile Info: Used only to confirm the identity of the authenticated account during the OAuth flow. This data is not stored persistently.
• Usage Logs: Used for internal debugging and monitoring purposes only. Logs are stored locally and not shared.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by the Service is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any cloud servers that store your personal data. OAuth tokens are stored in a local configuration file and are protected by your device's access controls. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect against unauthorized access, but no method of storage is completely secure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information only in the following circumstances:

• When required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority;
• To protect the rights, property, or safety of history-pov, our users, or others.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained locally until you manually delete them or revoke access through the respective platform's settings. Usage logs are retained locally and can be deleted at any time by the user. We do not retain any personal data on external servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

• Access: You may access any data stored locally by the Service at any time.
• Deletion: You may delete your local data (tokens, logs) at any time by removing the relevant files from your device.
• Revocation: You may revoke the Service's access to your third-party accounts at any time through the respective platform's settings page.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.
