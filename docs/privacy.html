Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how history-pov ("the App") handles information when you use it to publish videos to TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores only the OAuth access token and refresh token required to authenticate with TikTok on your behalf. No personal data beyond this is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Information

The stored tokens are used solely to upload and publish video content to your TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Token data is stored locally on your device only.

4. TikTok API

This App uses the TikTok API. By using the App, you also agree to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Token data is stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the token file from your device.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions, contact us via the GitHub repository associated with this project.
